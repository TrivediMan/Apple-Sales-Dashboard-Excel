🍎 Project Overview: Apple Sales Performance DashboardThe Apple Sales Performance Dashboard is a comprehensive data analysis project that tracks the global sales performance of Apple's flagship product lines. 
It transforms raw transactional data into high-level business intelligence, providing a detailed look at revenue streams, regional demand, and product model popularity.

✨ Key Analytical FeaturesGlobal Revenue Tracking: Provides a consolidated view of total revenue, reaching a grand total of ₹531,255,808 across all analyzed regions.Regional Performance Benchmarking: Compares sales across major markets:Australia: Leading region with ₹119,732,129 in revenue.Canada & UK: Strong secondary markets, both exceeding ₹114M in revenue.India: Growing market contributing over ₹100M.USA: Strategic market with ₹82.3M in reported revenue.Product Line Analysis:Categorizes performance into three core segments: Laptops (MacBook), Phones (iPhone), and Tablets (iPad).Tracks volume through "Units Sold" metrics to identify consumer preferences in different territories.Advanced Model Insights: Breaks down revenue by specific product models, such as the MacBook Air, iPhone 15, and iPad Pro, to highlight top-performing hardware.Financial Logic & Formulas: Implements complex Excel logic, including XLOOKUP, Index & Match, and Conditional Revenue tagging (e.g., "High" vs. "Low" revenue labels) to automate data classification.


🛠️ Technical SpecificationsFormat: Multi-sheet Excel workbook (.xlsx) with modular CSV exports.Data Integrity: Includes dedicated cleaning processes to ensure accuracy in pricing (INR) and unit calculations.Analytical Tools: Utilizes Pivot Tables for regional summaries and cross-tabulation of products by country.
